Amplify Hudson


media platform as a catalyst for an inclusive community

Warren Street, Hudson, NY
Collaborators Ryan Biswan Pryandana, Mariam Hattab, Berke Kalemoglu, David Mauricio

In order to encourage inclusive ownership of place, Amplify Hudson partners with local news sources and community organizations to mobilize citizens as advocating authors, editors, and producers of their own media. Through a new Media Workshop at the west end of Warren Street, participatory journalism and education empowers individuals to contribute their voice to democratic representation. A Civic Platform, east of Front Street, also invites people into the conversation with transparent government meeting spaces and an open plaza for broadcasting. Engagement in the collective input and the amplified output of local voices will connect the City of Hudson to the waterfront and allow residents to equitably reclaim the main street of Hudson as an inclusive place.
