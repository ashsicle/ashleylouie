2019-20 Magic Grant Posters


patent inspired digital poster design

Media digital image

Poster design for the 9 Magic Grant and the 4 Seed Grant projects awarded by The Brown Institute for Media Innovation at Columbia University and Stanford University. The collaborative design of the posters was inspired by patent drawings of objects related to each project topic.

The Brown Institute provides year-long funding awards for tools and stories that expand the practice for both engineering and journalism. For further information on the Magic Grant projects, refer to The Brown Institute for Media Innovation.
