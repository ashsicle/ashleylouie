NYC Organic Waste 2040


supply a circular economy within a local network

New York City, NY
Collaborators Donghanyu An, Alexandra Burkhardt, Xiaofei Sun, Dian Yu

Although the New York City Department of Sanitation lists the neighborhoods that receive curbside pick-up for organic waste, this collection service is only available to residents in single-family homes and buildings with less than 10 apartments. The reality is that many of these neighborhoods remain underserved with limited access to convenient drop-off locations.
