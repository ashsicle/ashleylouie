About


My name is Ashley Louie. I am an urban designer, analytical thinker, and creative coder with a passion for improving cities, engaging people, and serving communities. For me, the intersection of data visualization, cartography, and urban design provides an opportunity to understand invisible spatial relationships and to draw visible insights that can impact people and our environments.

With a background in architecture and urban design, I have developed a multi-scalar approach to understanding systems and spaces within the public realm. My work includes projects that visualize spatial data, connect urban fabric, and support social advocacy. I also love typography and occasionally freelance graphic design.
