ABOUT
My name is Ashley Louie. I am a designer and spatial thinker with a passion for public and private spaces that connect people and serve communities. For me, the intersection of data visualization, cartography, and urban design provides an opportunity to understand invisible spatial relationships and to draw visible insights that can impact people and our environments.

With a background in architecture and urban design, I have developed a multi-scalar approach to understanding systems and spaces within the public realm. My work includes projects focus on illustrating spatial data, connecting urban fabric, and supporting social advocacy.
